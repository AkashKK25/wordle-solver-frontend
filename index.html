WORDLE SOLVER
Instructions:
  1. Try a word in the WORDLE game.
  2. Enter the word here.
  3. Click on each letter cell to cycle through states:
    • Gray (absent): Letter is not in the word
    • Yellow (present): Letter is in the word but in the wrong position
    • Green (correct): Letter is in the correct position
  4. Click 'Solve'.
  5. Repeat until you get the correct word.

Enjoy!


Keyboard Shortcuts:
  • Press 'Enter' or 'Return' for 'Solve'
  • Press 'Delete' for 'Reset'
